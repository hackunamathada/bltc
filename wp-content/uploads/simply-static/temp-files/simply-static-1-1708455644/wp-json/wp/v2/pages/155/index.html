Life Transforming College International (LTCI) is a theological institute established in the year 2010 with the purpose of training and equipping born-again believers in Christ for the Great Commission. Our student body hails from Bangladesh, Bhutan, Myanmar, Nepal and India. It is a major program of Leadership Training Centre (LTC). Presently, the college offers two degrees: A Bachelor of Theology program (accredited to ATA) and a Master of Divinity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We aim to equip committed men and women with sound Biblical doctrines without compromising the inerrancy of the Word of God. We maintain a qualitative theological education with special emphasis on missions. Our students are privileged to be able to participate in various ministry activities of LTC. This gives them an edge over others because they are already equipped with the leadership skills necessary to step directly into the harvest field.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
