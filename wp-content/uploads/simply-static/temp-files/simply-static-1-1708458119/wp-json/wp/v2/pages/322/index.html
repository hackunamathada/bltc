IMPHAL, MANIPUR<\/strong><\/h5>\n\n\n\n

Our initial headquarter was based in Imphal and has seen many capable leaders at its helm. It is currently led by T. Ramananda Meitei as the Associate Director. He and his wife and two children live on campus in Tarung, Imphal. He oversees the Institute of Community Transformation which trains community leaders and equips them with the Word of God and practical skills to be able to plant self-propagating churches.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

SOUTH INDIA & SRI LANKA<\/strong><\/h5>\n\n\n\n

Our thriving ministry in South India and Sri Lanka is lead by Rev. V. John Muthuraj. He also serves as the regional coordinator for DCI. He continues to train and disciple many church leaders while shepherding his own flock at Eternal Life Assembly, Madurai. He lives with his wife and two sons in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. They have started a branch church in nearby Valayankulam and carry out ministry in 9 other villages conducting children\/youth camps and discipleship training.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

NEPAL<\/strong><\/h5>\n\n\n\n

Pastor Bhim Deuba<\/strong> leads the LTC team in Nepal and serves as the DCI regional coordinator as well. Pastor Bhim lives with his wife and two children in the town of Itahari in Nepal. He is a full time pastor involved in evangelism and church planting. Following in the tradition of the great reformers, he preaches in the open air and is also a sought after convention speaker. He has planted four churches in Itahari, Beldangi and Kailali with two evangelists working under him.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

ARUNACHAL PRADESH & MYANMAR<\/strong><\/h5>\n\n\n\n

Pastor Yongkon Tsupa<\/strong> lives in Nongtham, Arunachal Pradesh with his wife and three kids. He is actively involved in evangelism and church planting. Pastor Yongkong has successfully planted 12 churches, 6 of which have been handed over to the local associations. The remaining 6 are still under his care with the help of one evangelist and five voluntary lay leaders.
1. Arunachal Pradesh:<\/strong> Longpha, Dongronn, Nogtham, Chinsa, Jotinkhaike, Kaikhepothar, Kharsang, Wakhethna, Jungli, and Nongtham Adivasi church.
2. Myanmar:<\/strong> Longpha, Dongronn, Nogtham, Chinsa, Jotinkhaike, Kaikhepothar, Kharsang, Wakhethna, Jungli, and Nongtham Adivasi church.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

BANGLADESH<\/strong><\/h5>\n\n\n\n

Brother Dilip Singha<\/strong> lives with his wife in Molvi Bazar, Bangladesh. He carries on his work of evangelism, church planting and discipleship as the LTC and DCI regional coordinator. He reaches out to the locals through social activities such as free tuition for children and adult literacy programs. He has started three house churches in Noyapatton, Vanubil Mojergaon and Songaon.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
