The genesis of the Leadership Training Centre is a story deeply intertwined with the unwavering dedication and visionary leadership of Reverend Peter Kashung and his wife Moala. Their journey began with a shared educational experience at the esteemed Dallas Theological Seminary in the United States, where they not only gained knowledge but also ignited a passion for service that would shape their lives profoundly.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

After completing their studies, Peter and Moala embarked on a remarkable journey of service, guided by their faith and fueled by a desire to make a difference in the world. Their path led them to Manipur Theological College, where they dedicated thirteen years of their lives to nurturing and shaping the minds of future leaders. Peter served as Principal, while Moala imparted her wisdom as a Lecturer, leaving an indelible mark on the institution and its students.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Yet, amidst their tenure at Manipur Theological College, Peter and Moala sensed a stirring within their souls\u2014a divine calling to expand their ministry beyond the confines of academia and into the realm of evangelism and church planting. It was a calling they couldn’t ignore, a summons from a higher power compelling them to step out in faith and obedience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In response to this divine prompting, Peter and Moala embarked on a new chapter of their lives, founding the Leadership Training Center in 1993. This initiative was not merely the realization of a dream; it was a manifestation of their unwavering commitment to fulfilling the Great Commission of Jesus Christ\u2014to go forth and make disciples of all nations.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

For twenty-five years, the Leadership Training Center has stood as a beacon of hope and empowerment, spanning across six countries and impacting countless lives. Through its comprehensive programs and steadfast commitment to excellence, the center has equipped individuals with the skills, knowledge, and spiritual fortitude needed to effectively spread the message of Christ and lead others in faith.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

As we reflect on the remarkable journey of Reverend Peter Kashung and Moala, we are reminded of the transformative power of faith, obedience, and service. Their legacy serves as an inspiration to us all, urging us to heed the call of our own divine summons and make a lasting impact on the world around us.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"

Responding faithfully to this divine summons, the couple initiated the Leadership Training Center in 1993.
