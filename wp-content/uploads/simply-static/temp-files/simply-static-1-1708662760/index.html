“LTC Empowerment Story”

The genesis of the Leadership Training Centre traces back to the dedicated ministry of Reverend Peter Kashung and his wife Moala, who, having both received their education at Dallas Theological Seminary in the United States, embarked on a profound journey of service. Having spent thirteen years at Manipur Theological College, with Peter serving as Principal and Moala as a Lecturer, they discerned a divine calling toward evangelism and the establishment of churches. Responding faithfully to this divine summons, the couple initiated the Leadership Training Center in 1993.
Over the course of twenty-five years, spanning across six countries, the Leadership Training Center has remained steadfast in its mission to prepare and empower individuals with unwavering dedication to carry out the Great Commission of Jesus Christ.
